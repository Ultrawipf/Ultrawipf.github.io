177  - Action-Pad 4 buttons in diamonds-shape (on the right side) NORTH, SOUTH, WEST and EAST.
178  - D-Pad (Direction-pad) 4 buttons (on the left side) that point up, down, left and right.
179  - Menu-Pad Different constellations, but most-times 2 buttons: SELECT - START.
180  - Analog-Sticks provide freely moveable sticks to control directions, Analog-sticks may also

182  - Triggers are located on the upper-side of the pad in vertical direction. The upper buttons
